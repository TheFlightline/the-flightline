Navy Blvd Could Be Renamed to Honor 2019 NAS Pensacola Attack Victims — The Flightline

SB 628, filed by Sen. Don Gaetz, would designate a stretch of Navy Boulevard in Escambia County as 'Warrior Sacrifice Way' — honoring the three sailors killed in the December 2019 terrorist attack at Naval Air Station Pensacola.
