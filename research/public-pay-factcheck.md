## Reubin Askew biographical material

Primary sources: Florida Department of State (dos.fl.gov), Wikipedia (citing FL DOS), Tallahassee Democrat obituary, Florida Commission on Ethics Sunshine Amendment guide

VERIFIED:
- Born Sept 11, 1928, Muskogee, Oklahoma
- Mother moved with him to Pensacola in 1937
- Sold magazines, bagged groceries, shined shoes, sold his mother's homemade pies
- Pensacola High School graduate, 1946
- Korean War veteran (Air Force military intelligence)
- Florida State University (BS Public Administration, 1951)
- University of Florida law school (JD 1955)
- Assistant County Solicitor for Escambia County, 1956
- Florida House 1958, Florida Senate 1962
- Governor 1971-1979 (37th)
- Sunshine Amendment passed 1976 by 78% (Tallahassee Democrat says 80%; majority of sources say 78%; chose 78% as more-cited)
- Government in the Sunshine Law: passed 1967, NOT by Askew (he was state senator at the time)
- Death March 13, 2014
