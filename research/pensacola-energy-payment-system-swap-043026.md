## Background facts (each must trace to source)

### The outage
- Current Infinity Link online payment system taken down close of business Thursday, April 30
- New site available 8 a.m. Monday, May 4
- Customers cannot make online payment May 1 through 8 a.m. May 4
- No late fees assessed during this window
- Even if customers access new system over weekend, payments won't process
  → confirmed in: press release

### The new system
- Powered by MyMeter and InvoiceCloud
- Available at "Pensacola Energy | Pay My Bill" at 8 a.m. May 4
- New features:
  - More payment types (Apple Pay, Venmo)
  - 24/7 account access
  - Security updates
  - Easier account management
  - Automatic payments setup
  → confirmed in: press release

### Quote attribution
- Darryl Singleton, Pensacola Energy and Sanitation Services Director for the City: "The new payment system brings things up to date for customers who want to use platforms like Apple Pay and Venmo to pay their bills. It will make it easier for customers to get to their accounts and access what they need, at times that are convenient to them."
  → confirmed in: press release. ATTRIBUTABLE QUOTE.
