### About the Planning Board
- Established by Escambia County Ordinance No. 88-15
- Modified by Ordinance 2004-9 (adopted 2/5/2004) per F.S. 163.3174 and section 2.01 of the Escambia County Comprehensive Plan
- Functions as the local planning agency of Escambia County
  → confirmed in: press release

### Authority and duty (3 main areas)
1. Comprehensive planning — preparing the Comprehensive Plan / amendments, holding public hearings, recommendations to BCC
2. Land development regulation — public hearings on LDC text amendments, recommendations to BCC re: consistency
3. Quasi-judicial hearings — public hearings on rezoning, vested rights, planned unit developments
  → confirmed in: press release

### Membership composition
- 7 voting members total: 5 appointed by individual commissioners (one each), 2 at-large appointed by BCC
- 2 ex officio (non-voting): one appointed by Escambia County School Board, one jointly appointed by commanding officers of NAS Pensacola and NAS Whiting Field
- Voting members elect chair and vice-chair from among themselves; 2-year terms
  → confirmed in: press release

### Qualification requirements
- Must reside in Escambia County
- Cannot be a paid or elected county employee
- Must furnish resume / curriculum vitae to County Administrator and BCC
  → confirmed in: press release

### Service rules
- Commissioner-appointed members: 4-year terms concurrent with appointing commissioner
- At-large: 2-year staggered terms
- School board / Navy reps serve until replaced
- Removal: any commissioner can remove their own appointee; at-large can be removed by majority BCC vote
- 4+ absences in 12 months = automatic removal unless excused by chair
  → confirmed in: press release

### Meeting rules
- Quorum: at least 4 of 7 voting members
- Majority of those present required for any official action
- Public meetings under Florida Sunshine Law
- Robert's Rules of Order for procedure not otherwise covered
  → confirmed in: press release
